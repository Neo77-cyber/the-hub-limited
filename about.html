About Us

The Hub Terminus Limited was started to enable Micro Small & Medium Enterprises, independent Business owners, Brands grow sales, profit and fulfilment proficiency. Coming from years in Managing Transportation, Product Distribution and Fulfilment Services within the Logistics Value Chain at executive level, we aim to provide seamless Order Fulfilment, as the core Service with low cost and Best-In-Class Service. This is in view of improving the scalability rate of SMEs Pan-Nigeria and globally We seek to disrupt the logistics space with nimble cloud-based technology that will grow sales, optimize operations for multiple brands/vendors and delight end users (this service is Niche)

We help e-commerce vendors and Direct To Customer (D2C) brand owners with fulfilment while the focus on their business by generating sales, We Receive your stock, fulfil and distribute Pan Nigeria and Internationally while you grow your business. Our 3PL services are bespoke to your business needs, as we understand that every eCommerce business is unique from product preparation-Delivery; Pay-As-You-Go Storage, Pick, Pack, Label, Delivery and Returns Management. With reliable fulfilment Services and connected End-End Technology that powers our fulfilment network, our aim is to simplify storage, improve transit times, shipping cost and the delivery experience for your customers.

Our Fulfilment centre is strategically placed in the heart of Lagos mainland, within close proximity to Distribution Hubs, Vendors, Sea, Air and Land Ports. We are specifically designed for fast-moving consumer goods/brands, managed by our state of the art ”Fulfilment Software” and our team of professionals

Our Philosophy People. Process. Technology

People At The Hub Terminus Limited people are our most valuable asset. We encourage a culture of freedom and grow with the company by providing training, mentoring, and career development. We provide a structured, yet fun workplace for our fulfillment centre and office teams with a focus on embodying our corporate values of integrity, teamwork, innovation and excellence.

Process A lean, efficient process is KEY to high volume fulfilment. From pickup to delivery, each step in the process must ensure accuracy, visibility, and effectiveness and strong quality assurance process to back it up. Continuous improvement is a guiding principle at THL, as we strive to improve our processes and procedures to serve our clients with unmatched service excellence.
